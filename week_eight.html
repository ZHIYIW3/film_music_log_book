Making Music for Film and Animation 1 - Week 8 Log Book

Lecture Content and Reflection

The Magnificent Seven (1960)

  • The theme is introduced from the beginning and repeated at the back, a classic Western genre.
  • The opening has a strong, heavy texture, and rhythmic orchestration with characters riding the horse.
  • Strings play the fast tempo melody to set off the glorious scene.
  • There's a smooth transition to the slow tempo sadness, with a tender tone when characters communicate.
  • Using field recording and found sounds rather than using traditional instruments (percussion, brass, woodwinds, strings).
  • The music dives into the emotions of characters and what's going on, utilizing all the advantages, capturing the emotions in the music.
